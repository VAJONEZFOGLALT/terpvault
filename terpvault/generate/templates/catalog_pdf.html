Quick Guide

CDT
Cannabis-derived terpenes. Sourced directly from cannabis plants. Most authentic aroma replication. Premium pricing.
BDT
Botanical-derived terpenes. Extracted from non-cannabis plants. Globally compliant, cost-effective at scale.
HDT
Hemp-derived terpenes. Extracted from industrial hemp. Middle ground between CDT authenticity and BDT compliance.
Live Resin
Full-spectrum profiles preserving the fresh-frozen plant profile. Captures volatile compounds lost in curing. Premium vape.
Oil Soluble
Formulated for oil-based products — vape carts, edibles, and topicals. Requires a carrier oil for proper suspension.
Isolates
Single-molecule terpenes (99%+ purity). Used for precise formulation, custom blending, and reference standards.
Liquidisers
Thinning agents that reduce extract viscosity for use in standard vape cartridges. Blended with active ingredients.
Diluents
Carrier fluids for precise terpene dilution and custom blending. Provide consistent potency across production runs.
